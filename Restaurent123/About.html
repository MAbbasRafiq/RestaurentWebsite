About us

Nestled in the city of Gardens, situated at the rooftop of one of the highest building around liberty and offers Indoor & Outdoor Dining with an extensive view of Lahore. Eat Healthy Lahore is the definition of contemporary Restaurant, serving the finest Cuisines with a combination of traditional and exciting flavors from around the world.
            Since 2010, Eat Healthy Lahore has been serving its customer with Fine dining experiences. It has become the arena of restaurant industry with consistently pleasurable dining in an elegant atmosphere and refined sophistication.
            At Eat Healthy we take pride in offering an authentic Pakistani cuisine that is as rich and diverse as our legacy.
            It has been produced by the fusion of indigenous flavors with culinary heritage from Arabia, Persia and Central Asia. Fine dining at Eat Healthy is a 10/10 experience that never fails to impress its customers whether it’s the Sunday brunch, Hi-Tea or a Dinner with your family. Eat Healthy is built with the most up-to-date utilities while preserving a sense of culture & authenticity.
